Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Do not break any rule. Ban hammer will be ready if you break one.

Minecraft server

  1. • No Bypassing Chat Filter.
  2. • No IRL Trading.
  3. • No DDosing, Doxing, or Swatting.
  4. • Do not abuse unintentional bugs.
  5. • No Advertising. (livestreams can be posted in chat)
  6. • No Blacklisted Modifications (Anything that gives you a unfair advantage)
  7. • Be respectful and welcoming to all players.
  8. • Do not disrespect our staff team.

Discord server

  1. • No Advertising or Promotion.
  2. • No Racism, Discrimination or Hate Speech.
  3. • No Death Threats & Suicide Encouragement
  4. • Use common sense.
  5. • No sort of racism, hate-speech, bigotry, sexism or threatening behavior in any way.
  6. • No sort of toxicity. (be welcoming to others in our community!)
  7. • No malicious links, it will result in punishment.
  8. • No Impersonation of Staff

More rules

  1. • No Attempted Duplicating.
  2. • No Finding or Using the Seed.
  3. • No Punishment Evasion.
  4. • No Harassing & Abusing Others.
  5. • No Cross-Server Trading.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
